About the Podcast

Amir's Podcast is a platform dedicated to real conversations with real leaders—people who have built, fought, sacrificed, and earned their place through discipline, courage, and vision.

Hosted by Amir Ardebily, a 7th Degree Black Belt, entrepreneur, combat sports pioneer, and community leader, the podcast brings together a diverse range of guests including fighters, business owners, public officials, law enforcement professionals, medical experts, and influential voices from both the local and international stage.

With over five decades of experience in martial arts, leadership, and mentoring, Amir approaches every conversation with authenticity, respect, and a deep understanding of what it means to overcome adversity.

Amir's Podcast is not about hype. It is about truth.

Each episode explores the real stories behind success—stories of struggle, resilience, failure, and ultimately, triumph. Guests share lessons learned from their journeys, insights into leadership, and perspectives on the challenges shaping our world today.

In addition to entrepreneurship, leadership, and combat sports, the podcast also addresses cultural identity, global events, freedom, and the responsibility each generation carries to shape the future.

Based in St. Petersburg, Florida, Amir's Podcast reflects both a local foundation and a global perspective, connecting the Tampa Bay community with voices and ideas that matter.

At its core, Amir's Podcast exists for one purpose:

To give a voice to those who have earned the right to be heard—and to inspire the next generation to build strength, character, and purpose.

Featured Guests

Mayor Rick Baker

Former mayor of St. Petersburg, Florida, who served two terms from 2001 to 2010 and played a major role in the city's modern revival. With a background in law and business, Baker focused on downtown revitalization, education, and community-driven growth, leaving a lasting impact on the city's trajectory.

David Bybee

3x World Kickboxing Champion, CEO of American Mixed Martial Arts, and Director of Coaches for USA Kickboxing. A 6th Degree Black Belt in JLFS with a BJJ Black Belt under the DeLaRiva lineage, David's 36-year journey spans training under legends and shaping National and IKF Champions.

Nick Cornilus

A black belt and one of the Academy's most respected instructors, Nick is also the ISKA Super Heavyweight Kickboxing Champion who proudly represented the United States against Germany. His dedication to the sport and mentorship of the next generation make him a cornerstone of the martial arts community.

Russell Ricaldi

Retired federal agent and founder of ITAC Services, with 25 years of Federal Law Enforcement experience. Russell served as a SWAT Team Leader for HSI Tampa for over a decade and as an Instructor with the Department of State, Diplomat Security Service. His company provides advanced tactical training and personal protection.

Mike Yanez

A survivor, builder, and five-stripe black belt under the legendary Popovitch lineage, Mike represents the highest level of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Based in Miami, Florida, he has lived the martial arts life fully—the grind, the setbacks, the victories, and the responsibility of passing knowledge forward.

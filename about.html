Amir’s Podcast is a platform dedicated to real conversations with real leaders—people who have built, fought, sacrificed, and earned their place through discipline, courage, and vision.

Hosted by Amir Ardebily, a 7th Degree Black Belt, entrepreneur, combat sports pioneer, and community leader, the podcast brings together a diverse range of guests including fighters, business owners, public officials, law enforcement professionals, medical experts, and influential voices from both the local and international stage. With over five decades of experience in martial arts, leadership, and mentoring, Amir approaches every conversation with authenticity, respect, and a deep understanding of what it means to overcome adversity.

Amir’s Podcast is not about hype. It is about truth. Each episode explores the real stories behind success—stories of struggle, resilience, failure, and ultimately, triumph. Guests share lessons learned from their journeys, insights into leadership, and perspectives on the challenges shaping our world today. In addition to entrepreneurship, leadership, and combat sports, the podcast also addresses cultural identity, global events, freedom, and the responsibility each generation carries to shape the future.

Based in St. Petersburg, Florida, Amir’s Podcast reflects both a local foundation and a global perspective, connecting the Tampa Bay community with voices and ideas that matter. At its core, Amir’s Podcast exists for one purpose: To give a voice to those who have earned the right to be heard—and to inspire the next generation to build strength, character, and purpose.
